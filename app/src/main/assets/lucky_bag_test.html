• What is Lucky Bag?

    Lucky Bag contains a bundle of rubies. Anyone who sees a Lucky Bag can open it and will get a random amount of rubies. You can send Lucky Bag when your favorite VJ is live and anyone in the room can open it.

  • How to send Lucky Bag?

    1. Tap the Lucky Bag icon at the bottom of the screen.

    2. Choose the total amount of rubies and how many parts you want them to be divided.

    3. Make sure you have sufficient rubies and tap SEND button.

  • How to get rubies in the Lucky Bag?

    Tap to open the Lucky Bag once you see it and then you may get a random amount of rubies. Of course sometimes you may get none in bad luck. Notice that VJ will get Moons instead of Rubies.

  • What if there are still some remaining rubies in one lucky bag when the live come to an end?

    Don't worry. They will be returned to the one who sent them.
